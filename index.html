Making it in Hollywood: the dream of so many young, aspiring actors for nearly one hundred years. Millions have tried and few have succeeded, but those who have are the names that echo as synonyms for actors in the annals of history. Is it as easy as turning up and handing out your headshot on the Sunset strip, waiting to be discovered? Getting a job in a bar and cracking out Shakespeare monologues to every Tarantino wannabe? Sadly not, my friends. What it takes is work, skill, talent, drive, determination and a healthy dose of luck.
